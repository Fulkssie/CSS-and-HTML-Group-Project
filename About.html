Our History

Cofffe Haven was founded in Blountville, TN in 2010 by two longtime friends, Sarah and Michael. The idea for the coffee shop had been brewing for years, as both Sarah and Michael shared a passion for high-quality coffee and a desire to create a welcoming gathering place for their community.

After months of research, planning, and hard work, Coffee Haven opened its doors on a sunny Monday morning in August. From the beginning, the shop was a hit with locals and visitors alike, drawn to its warm atmosphere, delicious coffee, and friendly staff.

One of the keys to Coffee Haven's success was its commitment to sourcing the finest coffee beans from around the world. Sarah and Michael traveled to coffee farms in South America, Africa, and Asia, building relationships with farmers and learning about the different varieties and processing methods used to produce the perfect cup of coffee.

Back in Blountville, the Coffee Haven team roasted the beans on-site, carefully monitoring the temperature and timing to achieve the optimal flavor profile. Customers could choose from a variety of brewing methods, including pour-over, French press, and espresso, all expertly prepared by the skilled baristas.

But Coffee Haven was more than just a coffee shop. Sarah and Michael wanted to create a community gathering place where people could relax, connect, and enjoy a moment of respite from their busy lives. They hosted live music performances, poetry readings, and art exhibits, as well as offering a cozy space for book clubs and other groups to meet.

Over the years, Coffee Haven became a beloved fixture in the Blountville community, attracting a loyal following of regulars and earning accolades for its outstanding coffee and welcoming atmosphere. Sarah and Michael were proud to have created a business that brought people together and brought a little joy to their daily routines.

Today, Coffee Haven is still going strong, serving up delicious coffee and creating a warm and inviting space for people to come together. Sarah and Michael have expanded their offerings to include organic teas, pastries, and light meals, but their commitment to quality and community remains as strong as ever.
